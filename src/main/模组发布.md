# NoHurtRed - Remove Damage Tint

Have you ever felt that the red flash when an entity takes damage is distracting or ruins the game's visual immersion? **NoHurtRed** is a simple and lightweight mod that completely disables the red tint effect on entities when they are hurt.

## Current Features:

* Removes the red damage flash on all entities (players, mobs, animals, etc.).
* Highly compatible and lightweight.

# 没有受击变红模组 (NoHurtRed)

你是否觉得生物受伤时的红色闪烁效果十分刺眼，或者破坏了战斗时的沉浸感？**NoHurtRed** 是一个简单且轻量的模组，它的唯一作用就是完全禁用实体受伤时的红色渲染效果。

##### 目前提供的特性：

* 移除所有实体（玩家、怪物、动物等）在承受伤害时的红色闪烁效果。
* 轻量级代码，极佳的兼容性。
